Detailed information

 

Hack-A-Game in Matefest

The Hackathon will be organized as part of the main Matefest event and will be held that same day, April 26th from 8am to 10pm.

This implies it will be incompatible with having a stand on the Matefest.

Objective: A playabale game

To join the event you need a team of 4 people, with a limited number of 7 teams. Each team will have a unique theme to work in with a range of tools and engines freely chosen, and will compete amongst each other to make the best game ever.

The organization will suggest a game engine to work with, but each team it's free to pick their own one.

-

You will not be alone, Universitat de Barcelona will take along 3 professors.

About pre-requisits and previous knowledge

Some questions have arised on whether previous knowledge is mandatory, if there is any course restriction or which engine will be used.

We do not impose any restrictions for applying to the Hackathon, but of course places are limited and provable knowledge will play in your favour. Having done certain subjects might of course serve as a proof, but having personal projects will help too.

A selection process will be done, and final participants will be announced in March 2018.

Techonology/Engine

A range of engines and tools will be permitted. Each team will be able to choose the one that best fits their idea.

Worry not! All participants will be informed, via email and other means, one month or more before the Hackathon starts. This will ensure you have plenty of time to prepare

Thematic

You might be wondering how the theme will be chosen. We are going to keep it a secret to prevent any team having an advantage over the others.

To do so, all participants will propose themes during April. Those themes will be publicy viewable until te final election is made

From then on, the list of themes will be reduced, either by public votes or by election from the organizer team (professors only). Once only 3 themes last, no more polls will be done. The final theme will be revealed the very same day of the Hackathon, thus no team will be able to do work in advance.

What do I need?

The only thing you will need is a laptop, Universitat de Barcelona will provide both plugs and WI-FI. Please bear in mind, having a laptop is mandatory!

Frequently Ask Questions

Still have doubts? Here you will find some of the most asked questions and its answers.

When will I know if I'm in?

Participants will be made public on 23rd of March.

How will the teams be chosen?

The number of teams is limited to 7. If the number of teams registered in the Hackathon is greater than 7, the organization reserves its right to select the admitted ones.

Previous experience and motivation are powerful criteria, so don't forget to fill those fields carefully in the registration form.

Can I choose my team?

Yes, you can apply with a team. The team can either be complete/full or you can specify that you are still looking for people, in which case we will try to do so for you!

In the same way, you can register as solo and wait for a team to need you!

Do I have to be physically there?

Yes, only people registered, accepted and present might participate.

Does it cost anything?

No, it is completely free.

What should I bring?

You will only need your laptop and whatever tool you might use to work (paper, drawing pad...).
